## 🧰 How FounderOS Works

FounderOS uses a method called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide answers. Here’s what this means in simple terms:

- The app searches through stored documents and data that relate to your questions.  
- It picks the best matches to inform its AI responses.  
- This keeps answers accurate and based on relevant information, not guesswork.  

FounderOS connects to Supabase, an online service, to save your notes and conversations safely. This way, you can access your info across different devices.

The AI and workflow work together so you can get advice and organize your tasks in one smooth experience.
